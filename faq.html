Frequently Asked Questions

AddrSearch is a cloud-native address lookup service built on Google Cloud. We offer high-speed, secure tools for:

  • Fuzzy Address Search
  • GNAF (Geocoded National Address File) Search
  • Postcode Lookup

AddrSearch is powered by the official G-NAF dataset, maintained by PSMA Australia Limited under the Open GNAF licence agreement. It is the most authoritative and up-to-date dataset of physical addresses in Australia.

Our address data is refreshed quarterly in line with official GNAF releases from the Australian government.

AddrSearch is securely hosted on Google Cloud in Australia, with enterprise-grade reliability and uptime. Our serverless infrastructure ensures high availability and quick failovers.
